Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Crypto Drops, a mobile application designed to provide information about cryptocurrency airdrops. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

2. Information We Collect

Crypto Drops is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. You are not required to create an account or provide any personal details to use our app.

3. Data Collection and Use

AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve relevant ads, including device information, IP address, Advertising ID, and general location data. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Firestore: We use Firebase Firestore to store and manage the cryptocurrency airdrop data displayed in the app. This data does not include any personal information from users.

Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including app usage statistics, feature interaction data, and crash reports. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

4. Data Retention

We retain anonymized analytics data for a period of 14 months to help us with app improvement and trend analysis. After this period, the data is automatically deleted.

5. User Preferences

Our app offers both dark mode and light mode options. These settings are stored locally on your device and are not shared with or accessible by us.

6. Children's Privacy

Crypto Drops is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

Crypto Drops may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard practices to protect it. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Data Localization

The limited anonymized data collected through AdMob and Firebase Analytics is processed in accordance with Google's data practices. As Crypto Drops does not collect any personal information, there are no international transfers of personal data initiated by our app. For information about Google's data practices, please refer to their privacy policy.

10. Your Rights

You can choose to stop using our app at any time and control ad personalization through your device settings.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
